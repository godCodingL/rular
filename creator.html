Meet Our Team

Ayush Giri

Founder & Creator - NextMillan

Ayush Giri is the visionary founder of NextMillan and the mastermind behind Rural Skill Connect. With a deep passion for social impact and technology innovation, Ayush has dedicated his career to bridging the digital divide between urban and rural communities.

As a tech entrepreneur and social innovator, Ayush brings together cutting-edge technology with grassroots understanding to create solutions that truly make a difference in people's lives. His leadership and technical expertise have been instrumental in bringing Rural Skill Connect to life.

Key Achievements:

  • Founded NextMillan to drive digital innovation in rural communities
  • Developed Rural Skill Connect platform to empower rural workforce
  • Led multiple social impact technology initiatives across India
  • Recognized for innovative approaches to rural employment challenges
  • Mentored numerous young entrepreneurs in the social tech space
  • Published research on digital inclusion and rural development

Our Mission & Values

At NextMillan, we believe that technology should be a force for good, especially in empowering underserved communities. Our mission is to create innovative solutions that bridge the gap between rural talent and urban opportunities, ensuring fair wages and dignified work for all.

Social Impact

We prioritize creating positive change in communities over profit maximization.

Innovation
